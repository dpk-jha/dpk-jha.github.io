What the App Does

AfterCall Shield monitors when a phone call ends and automatically dismisses overlay screens — such as post-call pop-ups — that appear on your device immediately afterward. All detection and dismissal logic runs locally on your device in real time and produces no logs or records.

The app works out-of-the-box with zero configuration, automatically dismissing any non-system app overlay that appears in the brief window after a call ends.

The app operates as a background service so it can act the moment a call ends, even when the app is not in the foreground. A free tier allows up to 15 automatic dismissals; an optional one-time in-app purchase unlocks unlimited dismissals.

2

Permissions Used & Why

READ_PHONE_STATE

Detects when a call transitions to the idle state (call ended). This is the core trigger. The app reads only the call state (idle / ringing / off-hook) — it does not access phone numbers, call logs, IMEI, or any device identifiers.

Accessibility Service

Inspects the visible window immediately after a call ends to detect and dismiss overlay windows. The service is active only during a brief post-call window (5 seconds for answered calls, 8 seconds after unlock for missed calls). It does not read text you type, capture passwords, monitor clipboard content, or observe any activity outside of these post-call moments.

QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES

Enumerates installed apps so the autonomous mode can identify overlays and so you can build a personalised block-list or whitelist. This list is stored only on your device and is never transmitted or shared.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Displays a persistent notification required by Android to keep the background service running. No notification content is sent to any server.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE

Keeps the monitoring service alive in the background so overlay dismissal works reliably between calls, even when the app is not open.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Restarts the monitoring service automatically after a device reboot so you do not need to reopen the app manually.

REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS

Prompts you to exclude AfterCall Shield from battery optimisation, which prevents the operating system from killing the service during extended periods of inactivity.

Data Collection & Storage

AfterCall Shield does not collect, store remotely, or transmit any data. None of the following are ever accessed or recorded:

✓ No personal information
✓ No phone numbers or call logs
✓ No call duration or timestamps
✓ No device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID)
✓ No advertising ID
✓ No location data
✓ No contacts or address book
✓ No analytics or usage telemetry

Your dismissal counter, service state, and settings are saved on your device using Android DataStore. This data is backed up to Google Drive via Android Auto Backup when Google Backup is enabled on your device, allowing your usage counter and preferences to persist across reinstalls on the same Google account. Your block-list and whitelist (the apps you chose to monitor or exclude) are stored using Room (SQLite) and are not backed up — they will be cleared on uninstall. No data is ever transmitted to the developer's servers.

4

In-App Purchases

AfterCall Shield offers an optional one-time purchase (Unlimited Dismissals) processed entirely through the Google Play Billing system. The developer does not receive, store, or process any payment card details or billing information.

All financial transactions are handled by Google. Google's collection and use of data related to purchases is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. AfterCall Shield receives only a boolean signal confirming whether a valid purchase exists — no payment details are shared with the app.

The purchase status (premium unlocked: yes/no) is stored locally on your device and is used solely to remove the 15-dismissal free-tier limit.

5

Advertising

AfterCall Shield contains no advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDK or use the Android Advertising ID.

6

Third-Party Services

AfterCall Shield does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking services. The only external system the app communicates with is the Google Play Billing API — solely to verify and process in-app purchases as described in Section 4 above. No other data leaves your device.

7

Children's Privacy

AfterCall Shield is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no personal data from any user, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from minors.
