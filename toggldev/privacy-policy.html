TogglDev — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026  ·  v1.71

No personal data collected

Overview

TogglDev (com.dpkay.apps.developer_options_toggler) is an Android utility that lets you quickly enable or disable Android Developer Options from a home screen widget, Quick Settings tile, home screen shortcut, or the app itself.

This policy describes how Deepak Jha ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with the App. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

What the App Does

All processing happens entirely on your device. The App provides the following features:

Developer Options Toggle
Enable or disable Android Developer Options with a single tap, with full settings snapshot and restore.
USB Debugging Toggle
Enable or disable ADB over USB directly from the app.
Wireless Debugging Toggle
Toggle ADB over WiFi and see the connection IP:Port — tap to copy.
Animation Speed
Adjust window, transition, and animator scales (0×–2×) to speed up or slow down your device.
More Toggles
Show Touches, Stay Awake While Plugged In, Force RTL Layout, Don't Keep Activities, and Wait for Debugger.
Home Screen Widgets
5 widgets for one-tap control: Dev Options, USB Debugging, Wireless Debugging, open Developer Settings, and open Accessibility Settings.
Quick Settings Tiles
5 tiles in the notification shade for instant access to toggles and settings shortcuts.
App Shortcuts
Long-press the app icon to toggle Developer Options on or off.
Auto-Disable
Automatically turns off Developer Options when selected apps launch and restores them after a configurable delay.
17 Languages
Supports Arabic, Chinese, Danish, Dutch, French, German, Greek, Indonesian, Japanese, Korean, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Thai, and Turkish.
Battery Optimization
Optional exclusion from battery optimization to keep widgets and tiles responsive.

Any content from your device

Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permissions solely to perform its core functions:

WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS
Required to toggle Developer Options, USB Debugging (ADB), and animation scales on or off. This permission must be granted manually via ADB and is never used for any purpose other than writing developer-related system settings.
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS
Used to request that Android exempt the App from battery optimization. This is optional and user-initiated — it keeps the home screen widget and Quick Settings tile responsive in the background. Granting or revoking this is done entirely through Android's system Settings screen; the App initiates the system dialog only when you tap "Unrestricted Battery" in the App's Settings.
BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE
Used by the optional Auto-Disable feature. When enabled, the App runs an accessibility service that detects when selected apps come to the foreground and automatically turns off Developer Options so those apps see it as disabled. Developer Options are restored after you leave the monitored app. The service does not read, collect, or transmit any screen content, keystrokes, or personal data — it only observes which app is in the foreground. This service must be enabled manually by you in Android Settings and can be revoked at any time.
INTERNET
Used only by the Google Play Billing library to verify in-app purchases. No other network traffic is initiated by the App.
com.android.vending.BILLING
Used to process optional in-app purchases (tip/support the developer) through the Google Play Store. All billing is handled entirely by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment details.

Local Data & Storage

The App stores data only on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App. The following information is stored locally:

UI preferences — whether the Quick Settings tile has been added, snapshot expand state, etc.
Settings Snapshot — a copy of your developer settings captured before turning Developer Options off, including: USB Debugging state, window/transition/animator animation scales, Show Touches, and Stay On While Plugged In. These values are read back and restored when you re-enable Developer Options.
Auto-Disable app list — the list of apps you selected for automatic Developer Options disabling, and your chosen re-enable delay. No information about your app usage or launch history is stored.
Supporter status — whether you have completed an in-app purchase, cached locally to avoid repeated billing queries.

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing on our servers to retain or delete on your behalf.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases to support development ("Support TogglDev"). All transactions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase history is managed by your Google account.

A local flag is stored on-device to indicate supporter status so the App does not need to query Google Play on every launch.
