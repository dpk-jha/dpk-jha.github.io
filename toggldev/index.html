Features

Everything You Need.
One Tap Away.

Instant Toggle

Enable or disable Developer Options in one tap. No digging through Settings menus, no multi-step process.

Smart Snapshot

Every setting — animation speeds, USB debugging state, stay awake — automatically saved when you disable and restored when you re-enable.

Banking App Shield

Automatically turn off Developer Options when specific apps launch. Configurable delay before re-enabling. Your bank never knows.

USB Debugging

Toggle ADB on and off directly from TogglDev. No more navigating deep into Developer Options settings.

Wireless Debugging

Toggle wireless ADB with one tap. Connection IP and port displayed right in the app — tap to copy.

Animation Control

Set window, transition, and animator duration scales from 0x to 2x. One-tap 0.5x preset to make your device feel faster.

Accessibility Hide

Some banking apps crash when they detect an active accessibility service. Accessibility Hide temporarily removes TogglDev’s service while those apps are in the foreground.

Remote Access

Control developer settings from your desktop over your local network. Secured with 6-digit pairing and token auth. Companion app available.

App Rules

Create rules that apply settings to groups of apps. Force-enable, force-disable, or run custom actions when specific apps launch.

Command Automation

Define custom ADB/shell commands that run automatically on toggle events. Execution logs with timestamps, exit codes, and privilege detection.
