AutoDeck — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026  ·  v1.0.0

No personal data collected

Overview

AutoDeck (com.dpkay.apps.autodeck) is an Android app that lets you create named app lists ("drawers") and automatically switch between them based on triggers such as Bluetooth connections, Wi-Fi networks, location, time schedules, phone calls, NFC tags, and app notifications.

This policy describes how Deepak Jha ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with the App. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

What the App Does

All processing happens entirely on your device. The App provides the following features:

App Drawers
Create named lists of apps ("drawers" or "profiles") and switch between them with a tap or automatically.
Home Screen Widget
A Glance-based widget that shows the active drawer and lets you switch drawers directly from your home screen.
Bluetooth Triggers
Automatically activate a drawer when a specific Bluetooth device connects or disconnects.
Wi-Fi Triggers
Switch drawers when you connect to or disconnect from a specific Wi-Fi network.
Location Triggers
Activate a drawer when you enter or exit a geographic area (geofence).
Schedule Triggers
Set time-based rules to switch drawers at specific times or on specific days.
Call Triggers
Switch drawers when a phone call starts or ends.
NFC Triggers
Tap an NFC tag to activate a specific drawer.
Notification Triggers
Activate a drawer when a notification arrives from a specific app.
Multi-Condition Rules
Combine multiple trigger conditions (AND/OR) to create complex automation rules.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store:

Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
Device identifiers or advertising IDs
Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
Call logs, contact lists, or message content
Notification content from other apps
Browsing history or network traffic
Location history (geofence events are processed on-device and not stored beyond the active session)

Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permissions solely to perform its core functions. Each permission is tied to a specific trigger type and is only used when you enable that trigger:

BLUETOOTH · BLUETOOTH_CONNECT · BLUETOOTH_SCAN
Used by Bluetooth triggers to detect when paired Bluetooth devices connect or disconnect. The App reads device names to let you select which device to use as a trigger. No Bluetooth data is transmitted off your device.
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION · ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION · ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION
Used by location (geofence) triggers to detect when you enter or exit a geographic area. Location is processed on-device by Android's geofencing APIs. The App does not store location history or transmit your location to any server. Background location access is required so geofence triggers can fire when the App is not in the foreground.
ACCESS_WIFI_STATE · CHANGE_WIFI_STATE
Used by Wi-Fi triggers to detect which Wi-Fi network you are connected to. The App reads the network name (SSID) to match against your configured triggers. No Wi-Fi data is transmitted off your device.
READ_PHONE_STATE
Used by call triggers to detect when a phone call starts or ends. The App does not read phone numbers, call logs, or any call content — it only observes the call state (ringing, off-hook, idle).
NFC
Used by NFC triggers to detect when an NFC tag is tapped. The App reads the tag ID to match against your configured triggers. No NFC data is transmitted off your device.
BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE
Used by notification triggers to detect when a notification arrives from a specific app. The App reads only the source package name of the notification to match against your configured triggers. It does not read, store, or transmit notification content, message text, or any other notification data. This service must be enabled manually by you in Android Settings and can be revoked at any time.
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM · USE_EXACT_ALARM
Used by schedule triggers to fire drawer switches at specific times. Alarms are managed by Android's AlarmManager and processed entirely on-device.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
Used to re-register triggers (geofences, alarms, Bluetooth/Wi-Fi monitors) after the device restarts, so your automation rules continue to work without manually reopening the App.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE · FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION
Used to run a foreground service for Wi-Fi monitoring and location-based geofence triggers. A visible notification is shown while the service is active, as required by Android.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Used to show notifications for foreground services and trigger activity. No marketing or promotional notifications are sent.
QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
Used to list installed apps so you can select which apps to include in a drawer. The list of installed apps is never transmitted off your device.
INTERNET
Included in the manifest but not actively used by the App to transmit data. No network calls are made to our servers or any third-party service.

Local Data & Storage

The App stores data only on your device using Android Room (SQLite) and DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App. The following information is stored locally:

Drawers & app lists — the names and contents of drawers you create, and which drawer is currently active.
Trigger rules — your configured automation rules (Bluetooth device names, Wi-Fi SSIDs, geofence coordinates, schedule times, etc.).
Trigger session state — which triggers are currently active, used to evaluate multi-condition rules. Cleaned up automatically.
UI preferences — active drawer selection, widget state, and user preferences via DataStore.

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing on our servers to retain or delete on your behalf.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. All functionality is implemented using Android platform APIs.
