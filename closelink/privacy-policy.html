1. Introduction

Welcome to CloseLink ("we," "our," or "us"). CloseLink is a local peer-to-peer (P2P) audio calling and connectivity tool designed for direct communication between devices.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. CloseLink is built with a core architecture that ensures your communications remain local. The application functions as an offline utility tool and does not employ remote servers to store, transmit, or process your personal data, audio, or messaging history.

Key Point: CloseLink runs completely on your own device. The voice data and connection packets are sent directly to the device you pair with via local connections (like Bluetooth LE and local audio networks).

2. Device Permissions & How They Are Used

To provide peer-to-peer audio calling, device discovery, and call management features, CloseLink requests access to specific device hardware and system features. These permissions are used exclusively to enable the app's functionality on your device:

A. Bluetooth & Location-related Permissions

  • Bluetooth Scan & Bluetooth Advertise (android.permission.BLUETOOTH_SCAN, android.permission.BLUETOOTH_ADVERTISE): Used to broadcast your device's availability and scan for other nearby devices running CloseLink. We use the neverForLocation flag, indicating that we do not use Bluetooth to determine or track your physical coordinates.
  • Bluetooth Connect (android.permission.BLUETOOTH_CONNECT): Used to establish a direct connection between your device and a selected nearby device.

B. Audio & Microphone Permissions

  • Record Audio / Microphone (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO): Required to capture your voice during active calls so that it can be streamed directly to the connected peer. Audio is processed in real time and is never saved, cached, or recorded on the local device or sent to any central server.
  • Modify Audio Settings (android.permission.MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS): Allows the app to adjust sound levels, switch between earpiece/speaker modes, and optimize the sound profile for clear communication.

C. System & Phone Permissions

  • Read Phone State (android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE): Used to detect incoming cellular phone calls. This allows CloseLink to automatically pause or terminate local peer-to-peer voice sessions so you can answer incoming calls seamlessly. We do not read or record phone numbers, call histories, or contacts.
  • Foreground Service (android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_CONNECTED_DEVICE): Required to keep your local audio transmission and peer-to-peer connection stable in the background while you navigate out of the app. This is accompanied by persistent system notifications so you are always aware when a call is active.
  • Request Ignore Battery Optimizations (android.permission.REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS): Allows the app to stay connected during long sessions and prevents the Android OS from terminating the direct connection when the screen is turned off.
  • Wake Lock (android.permission.WAKE_LOCK): Prevents the device's processor from sleeping during an active voice session to ensure uninterrupted audio quality.

3. Third-Party Services & Analytics

We do not share any user information with third parties. CloseLink does not contain third-party software development kits (SDKs), advertising platforms, or tracking analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase SDKs) that gather data about your device, behaviors, or location.

Since no telemetry or diagnostics are gathered by us, no data is sold, traded, or distributed to third parties under any circumstances.

4. Data Security

CloseLink uses direct peer-to-peer communication channels. Your audio communications are transmitted directly between the connected devices. However, because the communication takes place over local peer connections, you should ensure that your Bluetooth connection and local environment are secure.

We do not store database back-ups, user profiles, or connection histories on any server, which eliminates the risk of cloud-based data breaches.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect, record, request, or store any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since CloseLink operates entirely as an offline, peer-to-peer communication tool, it complies fully with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR regulations regarding child safety and privacy.
