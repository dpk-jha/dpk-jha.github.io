Privacy Policy

LayOff: Call Overlay Cleaner  ·  Package: com.dpkay.apps.after_call_cleaner

Short version: LayOff works entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

1. What LayOff Does

LayOff monitors when a phone call ends and automatically dismisses any overlay screens (such as post-call pop-ups or app screens) that appear afterward. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Permissions Used & Why

  • READ_PHONE_STATE — Detects when a phone call transitions to the idle state (call ended). This is the core trigger for the overlay-dismissal feature. The app reads only the call state (idle / ringing / off-hook); it does not read phone numbers, IMEI, or any device identifiers.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Displays a persistent notification required by Android to keep the background service running while the app is active.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — Enumerates installed apps so you can build a block-list of apps whose overlays should be dismissed. The list of installed apps is stored only on your device and is never transmitted.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE — Keeps the monitoring service alive in the background so overlay dismissal works even when the app is not open.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Restarts the monitoring service automatically after the device reboots, so you do not need to reopen the app manually.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — Prompts you to exclude LayOff from battery optimization, which prevents the OS from killing the service during extended periods of inactivity.
  • Accessibility Service — Used to detect and dismiss overlay windows that appear after a call ends. The accessibility service only inspects the currently visible window structure; it does not read text you type, capture passwords, or monitor activity outside of post-call overlay detection.

3. Data Collection & Storage

LayOff does not collect, store remotely, or transmit any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No phone numbers, call logs, or call duration data are accessed or stored.
  • No device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID) are read or used.
  • Your block-list preferences are saved only in local app storage on your device using Android DataStore.

4. Advertising

LayOff contains no advertisements and does not use any advertising SDK or the Android Advertising ID.

5. Third-Party Services

LayOff does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising services. No data leaves your device.

6. Children's Privacy

LayOff does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
