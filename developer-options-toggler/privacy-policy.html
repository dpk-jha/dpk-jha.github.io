TogglDev — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

No personal data collected

Overview

TogglDev ("the App") is an Android utility that lets you quickly enable or disable Android Developer Options from a home screen widget, Quick Settings tile, or the app itself.

This policy describes how Deepak Jha ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with the App. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Any content from your device

Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permissions solely to perform its core function:

  • WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS — Required to toggle Developer Options on or off. This permission must be granted manually via ADB and is never used for any purpose other than writing the developer options system setting.
  • INTERNET — Used only by the Google Play Billing library to verify in-app purchases. No other network traffic is initiated by the App.
  • BILLING (com.android.vending.BILLING) — Used to process optional in-app purchases (e.g., tip/support the developer) through the Google Play Store. All billing is handled entirely by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment details.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases to support development. All transactions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase history is managed by your Google account.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party component:

  • Google Play Billing Library — Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

No analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or other third-party trackers are included.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. The App stores only your in-app preferences (such as UI settings) locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. This is a developer utility intended for technically proficient adult users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
